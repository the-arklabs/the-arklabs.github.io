Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CamDoc ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

CamDoc is designed as an offline-first utility application. All operations, including document scanning, PDF creation, and file manipulation, are performed locally on your device. We do not have servers that process your documents.

2. Permissions and Device Access

To function correctly, CamDoc requires access to certain features on your device. Here is why we ask for them:

Camera Access

  • Purpose: To scan physical documents and convert them into digital images/PDFs.
  • Data Handling: Images taken via the app are processed instantly on your device. They are not sent to any external server.

Storage / Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: To allow you to import images or PDFs for editing, and to save your created PDF files to your device.
  • Data Handling: The app only accesses files you specifically select or create.

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard operating system libraries and reputable open-source packages to perform functionalities (like PDF rendering or image scanning). These tools function locally on your device.

  • We do not use third-party analytics services (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics).
  • We do not display third-party advertisements (e.g., AdMob).

4. Data Security

Since your files and data are stored locally on your device, their security depends on your device's security settings (e.g., device passcode, biometric lock). We do not have access to your files.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email address, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
